Mobilize

company: Momentus Software
Description

Mobilize, previously called SalesTap, is a product that offers a centralized management solution for distributing marketing collaterals different teams and individuals of an organization. I have contributed to the project with the interface design/development and helped in defining the direction of the user experience with our business and development leads. My work covers for all platforms that Mobilize is offered in: web, native iOS and native Android.

Deliverables
  • wireframes
  • use flow alternatives
  • rapid prototype
  • hi-def mock-ups
  • guideline documents
  • front-end development

A lot of the design work was done in a fast-paced environment, where development and design took place at the same time. In order to accomodate to the fast-tracking process, quick-thinking was required, as well as front-end development. In a short period of time, I have also outputted guideline documents to align with a new sass model to replace our previous customization service, where I used to re-skin our base product with various clients' assets. This project required flowing discussions with the development and business team in meetings and also via Jira.
