Clap Clap Apps

agency: Momentus Software
Description

Clap Clap Apps is a company that creates and shares interactive stories on tablet devices, mainly for kids of South Asian descent, with the purpose of familiarizing them with Indian culture and speech.
For this project, I was entasked with creating a landing page even before creation of the product itself. This process demanded strong communication skills and a flexible work process in order to accomodate for the deadline that was imposed by the client, a team with members in different places/time zones.

Deliverables
  • user/market research
  • wireframes
  • prototype
  • user testing
  • branding
  • hi-def mocks

A lot of pre-production meetings were required to grasp and plan out the general look and feel that the company was looking for, as there were no graphical assets available when the landing page was first being built. With no content or graphics provided, I created wireframe alternatives from my own research on similar product/company sites.
This landing page required much attention on the cross-browser compatibility, as there were technical constraints that raised up while adding a heavy animation in the format of a spritesheet.
During the first few weeks of launch, I was also in charge of embedding and managing the Google Analytics, Newsletter Subscription via MailChimp and applying Optimization tentatives on the site and also providing assets for this company's social media.
