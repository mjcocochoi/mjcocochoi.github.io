Neo

school: VFS
Description

Neo is a fictional boutique hotel that brands itself as a local topshop hotel that promotes and reflects the local businesses and design community of Gastown. Part of my studies at VFSâ€™ Digital Design program, we were given a brief to create the brand identity for a new boutique hotel to be located in the Gastown district of Vancouver, BC. By performing a quick research on the area, I discovered that Gastown took pride in the diverse design and fashion culture that was exhibited by its small hidden shops, and decided to form my hotel concept around that finding.

Deliverables
  • user/market research
  • branding
  • hi-def mocks

As this project was completed as a course project throughout a whole branding course of 2 months, a lot of effort was put in user/market researching and creating an authentic brand.
This was a great opportunity to follow through a full process in creating a brand identity: from research, brand pyramid, personas and moodboards, sketches and color palettes, to creating the final logo and application mocks.
