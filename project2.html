K9 to K9

agency: Momentus Software
Description

For K9 to K9, we were first approached by a man with the name K9 to K9, a few sketches and a personal story. K9 to K9 is an app that has been created to satisfy its founder's hope and idea of creating a responsible community of dog owners that contribute together to prevent canine attacks. As a MVP, this first version of the product allows dog owners to register their dog's information which they can broadcast to other users while on walks, and keep note of every interactions by creating personal social lists.
